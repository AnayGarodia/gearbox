Currency amounts are handled in two files: src/money.ts defines `Money` and `addMoney`, and src/cart.ts uses them in `cartTotal`. Amounts are meant to be integer CENTS, but src/money.ts mixes dollars and cents: `fromDollars(1.10)` stores 1.1 instead of 110, so totals come out wrong and float-dirty. Fix the representation so `fromDollars` stores integer cents (round half up), `addMoney` stays in cents, and `cartTotal([{price: fromDollars(1.10), qty: 2}])` equals fromDollars(2.20) exactly.
