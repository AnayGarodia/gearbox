`parseCsvLine` in src/csv.ts handles plain fields but mangles quoted ones. Per RFC 4180: a field MAY be wrapped in double quotes; inside quotes, commas are literal and a doubled quote ("") is a literal quote character; quotes are stripped from the result; whitespace is preserved exactly. Examples: 'a,"b,c",d' => ["a", "b,c", "d"]; '"say ""hi""",x' => ['say "hi"', "x"]; 'a,,b' => ["a", "", "b"]; '""' => [""]. Unterminated quotes throw an Error. Fix it; keep the export.
